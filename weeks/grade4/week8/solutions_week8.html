Week 8 — Answers & Solutions

Grade 4 · Math Challenge
Problem 1
Who won the race?
Answer: Mindy
Mark all the racers on the line. Mindy finishes before Lindy, and Lindy finishes before Cindy, so the order so far is Mindy, Lindy, Cindy. Bob finishes second, which leaves Mindy in first. So Mindy won the race.
Problem 2
Which weight did Amelia set aside?
Answer: 1 kg
All six weights add up to 1+2+3+4+5+6 = 21 kg. After trying a few options, the only way the two pans balance is to set aside the 1 kg weight: that leaves 20 kg, so each pan holds 10 kg (5+3+2 = 10 on the left, 6+4 = 10 on the right).
Problem 3
In how many ways can you form the word WATER?
Answer: 6
Starting upward from W, there are 3 different paths to R.If you start downward instead, you get a symmetrical set of 3 paths. So altogether there are 3 + 3 = 6 ways.
Problem 4
How far is the park from Meera's house?
Answer: 2 miles
Meera walks to the park once and back home once, and then walks 2 miles with her dog. Taking away those 2 miles leaves 6 − 2 = 4 miles for the round trip to the park and back. Half of that is the one-way distance: 4 ÷ 2 = 2 miles.
Problem 5
How many pushups does Nathan do in 7 mornings?
Answer: 91
Simply multiply the pushups in one morning by the number of mornings: 13 × 7 = 91.
Problem 6
Which picture has different white and grey areas?
Answer: D
In four of the five pictures the grey area is exactly half, so the grey and white areas are equal. Only in picture D is there less grey than white, so its white and grey areas are different.
