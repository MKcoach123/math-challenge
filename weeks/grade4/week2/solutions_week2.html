Week 2 — Answers & Solutions

Grade 4 · Math Challenge
Problem 1
Anna is 6 years old. Her father will be 40 years old when Anna reaches the age of 10. How old is Anna's father?
Answer: 36 years old
Anna reaches 10 in 10 − 6 = 4 years. Her father will be 40 then, so now he is 40 − 4 = 36.
Problem 2
Nancy is 3 years older than Mary. Clark is 2 years younger than Nancy. Ben is 14 years old and twice as old as Clark. How old is Nancy?
Answer: 9 years old
Work backwards: Clark = 14 ÷ 2 = 7. Nancy = 7 + 2 = 9. (And Mary = 9 − 3 = 6.)
Problem 3
What is the value of the star?
Answer: 6
From ◉ + ❖ = 11, substitute into ◉ + ❖ + ★ = 17, so ★ = 17 − 11 = 6.
Problem 4
The picture shows the front of Tom's house. The back has 3 windows and no door. What does Tom see at the back?
Answer: E
When looking from the back, the house should have three windows and a chimney on the left side — so the answer is option E.
Problem 5
The solid is made of 8 cubes. How does it look from above?
Answer: C
Looking from the top, we should see two parts of the solid: one with two cubes and one with three cubes — so the answer is option C.
