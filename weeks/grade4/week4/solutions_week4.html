Problem 2
How many grams does a half-full glass weigh?
Answer: 250 g
The empty glass weighs 100 g, so the water in the full glass weighs 400 − 100 = 300 g. Half of that weighs 300 ÷ 2 = 150 g. So the half-full glass weighs 100 + 150 = 250 g.

Problem 4
How many squares balance the three triangles?
Answer: 6
Look carefully at the scales. 1 square and 1 triangle weigh the same as 6 circles. One triangle weighs the same as 4 circles, so 1 square weighs the same as 2 circles. That means each triangle is worth 2 squares, so 3 triangles weigh 3 × 2 = 6 squares.
Problem 5
How many eggs will 6 hens lay in 6 days?
Answer: 12
1 hen lays 1 egg in 3 days, so 1 hen lays 2 eggs in 6 days. So 6 hens will lay 6 × 2 = 12 eggs.
